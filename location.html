My Backyard

Where Am I?

East Pennsboro Township is in south-central Pennsylvania, across the Susquehanna River from Harrisburg the State capital.

Where I Work

Many of the things I do as an employee of my local government impact the services available to myself and other residents. I work for East Pennsboro Township in a triple capacity:

Title Description
Wastewater Superintendent Manage personnel and equipment at the Township's wastewater treatment plant and collecting pumping stations.
Stormwater Coordinator Keep an inventory of the Township's stormwater assets and activities, while maintaining compliance with the PA Department of Environmental Protection stormwater permit.
IT Specialist Plan maintenance and upgrades on tech assets across all Township work sites, along with project management, working with vendors and balancing a spending budget.
